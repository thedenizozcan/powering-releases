Powering
Powering is a macOS app that puts your favorite actions in a radial command wheel, giving you instant access to folders, apps, websites, and Shortcuts.

It's native, lightweight, and customizable. Once set up, it stays out of your way until you need it, ready to launch your commands with a double tap of the Option key.

Requirements
Powering requires macOS 14.0 or later

Key Features
Radial Command Wheel: Access your most-used actions from an intuitive circular menu that appears exactly where you need it.
Open Anything: Launch apps, open folders in Finder, or jump to websites instantly.
Shortcuts Integration: Run your Apple Shortcuts directly from the wheel for powerful automations at your fingertips.
Designed for The Mac: A fully native Mac app that feels right at home and adapts automatically to your appearance settings.
